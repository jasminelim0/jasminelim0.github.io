Recipe #1: TACOS

  • Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Coles Taco hard Shells 8
Beef Mince 500g
Mexico Chile Con Carne Seasoning 50g pkt
Shredded Iceberg Lettuce 2 cups
Red Capsicum (seeded, finely chopped) Small (200g)

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Place the taco shells on a large baking tray and bake for 5 minutes or until heated through

Step 2: Add the mince to a non-stick frying pan over high heat. Cook, stirring for 5 minutes or until the mince changes colour.

Step 3: Add the Carne Seasoning and 3/4 cup (180mL) of water. Cook, stirring for 5 minutes of until the mixture thickens.

Step 4: Divide the taco shells among serving plates and fill each shell with the mince mixture, lettuce and capsicum.

Recipe #2: MOZZARELLA STICKS

  • Time: 30 min for prep, 5 min to cook
  • Serves:6
Ingredients Quantity
Plain Flour 75g
Eggs (lightly whisked) 2
Panko Beadrcumbs 120g (2 and 1/2 cups)
Block Mozzarella 450g
Vegetable Oil Enough to shallow fry
Tomato Pasta Sauce Preference (to serve)
Pesto Dip Preference (to serve)

Step 1: Place flour, egg and breadcrumbs in separate shallow bowls.

Step 2: Line a baking tray with baking paper and cut the mozzarella into 1.5cm thick slices, then into sticks.

Step 3: Toss the sticks into the flour and shake off the excess. Then dip it into the egg, and finally roll into the breadcrumbs, pressing firmly to coat.

Step 4: Place in the fridge for 20 minutes to chill.

Step 5: Pour oil into a pan (enough so that it comes up 1cm off the side) and heat it over medium-high heat.

Step 6: Line a baking tray with a paper towel and begin shallow-frying the sticks in batches. Turn for 1 minute or until golden.

Step 7: After frying season with salt and serve warm with pasta sauce and pesto dip.

Source: Taste

Recipe 3: LEMON DESSERT

  • Time: 4 hours and 30 minutes
  • Serves:10
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 2 and 3/4 cups
Dutch Speculaas Cookies 16
Fresh Ricotta 1kg
Finely Grated Lemon Rind 1 and 1/2 tablespoons
Lemon Juice 2 tablespoons
Caster Sugar 2/3 cups
Vanilla Flavoured Dessert Mix 100g packet
Softened Lemon Butter 1/2 cup
Boiling Water 2 tablespoons
Gelatine Powder 1 tablespoon
Thickened Cream 300ml
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Lemon Slices Preference (to decorate)
Small Fresh Mint Leaves Preference (to decorate)

Step 1: Remove the milk from fridge and let it stand for 30 minutes to let it become room temperature.

Step2: Arrange biscuits in a single layer over the base of a 4cm-deep 20cm x 28cm (base) baking dish, trimming the biscuits if necessary.

Step3: Place ricotta, lemon rind, lemon juice and 1/2 cup sugar in a food processor and process until smooth and combined. Evenly spread ricotta mixture over biscuits and refrigerate until required.

Step 4: Make the vanilla dessert, using milk and following packet directions. Whisk in the lemon butter and place boiling water in a small bowl. Sprinkle over the gelatine and stir until it dissolves.

Step 5: Whisk gelatine into lemon mixture. Refrigerate for 15 minutes or until mixture thickens slightly.

Step 6: Gently pour lemon mixture over ricotta mixture. Refrigerate for 4 hours or until set

Step 7: Using an electric mixer, beat cream, vanilla and remaining sugar until just-firm peaks form. Spread cream over lemon layer. Decorate with lemon slices and mint leaves. Serve.

Source: Taste
